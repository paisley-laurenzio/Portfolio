Look At My Work

I am primarily a UI/ UX designer but like to work in print every now and then. I love to push the limits of creativity to solve communications problems and I am constantly learning and growing as a designer.

So who am I?

I have lived in Canada my whole life and becoming a designer was really a no brainer. As a kid I loved art and as I got older I realized I could incorporate that love into a career. So of course, I did! I am never the loudest person in the room and some say I have a very sarcastic sense of humor. I am openly a superhero nerd and love to go for runs in my free time and as a way to de-stress.
