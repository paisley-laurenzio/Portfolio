This app is no ordinary GPS. It is designed specifically for cyclists, providing hands free visual and audible hand signal prompts to increase their safety. The mobile device is mounted on the cyclistâ€™s handlebars where it is clearly in the users line of vision.

The app provides the user with two options once they have input the destination of a route. They can opt to either use the full GPS that shows their movement on the map and provides hand signal prompts or they can choose to use a fully audio version where the visual is just the hand signal prompts.

The app provides a favorites feature that allows the user to instantly save a new route for quick access in the future.
