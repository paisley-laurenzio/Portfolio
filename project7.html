--- layout: default title: Mybaby App Design ---

MYbaby is an app designed specifically for young mothers of unexpected and unplanned pregnancy. The app aims to help solve one of the United Nations Global Goals. MYbaby focuses on health and wellness by educating mothers so that they can ensure good health and well being for their babies.

MYbaby provides the mothers with a skill library that has a series of tutorials on baby care for them to work through during their pregnancy so that they are prepared and confident to care for their baby once their baby is born.
