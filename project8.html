--- layout: default title: Broadcast Package Design ---

This project is a motion graphics broadcast package. The package includes a show bumper, examples of how transitions from scene to scene would look and an example of what a lower thirds title would look like.

For the look and feel of this piece the goal was to depict the mountains data in a graphically pleasing and engaging way while keeping it sleek and polished. To achieve this a monochromatic blue colour pallet was used for the intro video with the graphics overlaid in white so that they blend, creating an overall unified look to the piece.
